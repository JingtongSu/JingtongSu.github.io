About me

Hi! I'm a senior undergraduate at Peking University, majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology. My research interests are in studying foundations of deep learning and designing algorithms based on theoretical insights and empirical observation. Recently, I was fortunate to join the Princeton ISIP program online and to spend a wonderful summer together with Prof. Jason D. Lee and a group of incredible teammates.
I will apply for a Ph.D. this winter. Please don't hesitate to contact me if you're interested!
